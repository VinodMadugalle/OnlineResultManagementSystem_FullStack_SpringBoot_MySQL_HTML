What is Online Exam An online exam is an assessment that is conducted over the internet, allowing students or test-takers to complete the test remotely, usually from their own computer or mobile device. Online exams can be administered through a variety of methods, including web-based applications, software programs, or learning management systems. Online exams can be used
for a variety of purposes, such as testing students' knowledge or skills, certifying professionals in a particular field, or assessing job applicants' suitability for a role. They may be timed or untimed, and can include different types of questions, such as multiple choice, true/false, or essay questions. Online exams are often used in distance learning programs or for remote workers who are unable to attend a physical testing center. Set up the Zoom meeting: Schedule a Zoom meeting and send the link and password to the participants who will be
taking the exam.

Communicate the exam format: Inform the participants about the format of the exam, such as the duration, type of
questions, and any materials they need to have ready.

Test the technology: Make sure all participants can access the Zoom meeting and that their audio and video are
working properly.

Explain the exam rules: Outline the rules for the exam, such as whether participants are allowed to use external
resources, how to submit answers, and the consequences of cheating.

Monitor the exam: As the participants take the exam, monitor the Zoom meeting to ensure that no one is cheating
or breaking the exam rules.

Collect the exam submissions: Once the exam is complete, collect the answers from the participants, either through
Zoom's chat or screen sharing features or through an external platform such as a learning management system.

Grade the exam: Use the grading rubric to evaluate the exam submissions, and provide feedback to the participants
as necessary.

Share the exam results: Share the exam results with the participants through email or an online platform, and answer
any questions they may have about the exam.
